Mail Server

Running my own mail server is pretty normal for me by now. Using gmail, yahoo or things like that never felt right to me. Our mail server offers a variety of domains and emails will always be stored securely.

NEW: I now also offer my own public tempmail service at 48hr.email!

DNS

A DNS server is vital in resolving your easy-to-remember URLs into the actual IPs the server listens to like 89.163.155.202 for example. Running one on your own is no easy task. Yet I tried myself on it, and its working very well, resolving roughly 15M requests every day, using my own upstream resolver.

Hacking, and specifically malware always fascinated me. Early on I decided that I want to understand how exactly these malicious files work, and how to detect them. Throughout my teens I have analysed, decompiled and even written a few myself as a proof-of-concept. Now I am writing my own Firmware called Xtreme for the Flipper Zero.
