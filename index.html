My Services & Products

Here is an overview of the services & products I currently run/provide

Cloud Service

I host my own cloud service, allowing people to store their files, manage their schedules using CalDav addons, create and edit spreadsheets, presentations & other documents, and many more things. The Cloud is perhaps my oldest still running project to date, having started before covid, around december 2018.

Mail Server

Running my own mail server is pretty normal for me by now. Using gmail, yahoo or things like that never felt right to me. Our mail server offers a variety of domains and emails will always be stored securely.

NEW: I now also offer my own public tempmail service at 48hr.email!

DNS

A DNS server is vital in resolving your easy-to-remember URLs into the actual IPs the server listens to like 89.163.155.202 for example. Running one on your own is no easy task. Yet I tried myself on it, and its working very well, resolving roughly 15M requests every day, using my own upstream resolver.

Secure Sharing

I run a secure sharing service, allowing you to share files with others, without the need to worry about your privacy. The service is encrypted end-to-end (Using client-side AES in GCM mode), and the files are only stored in ram on our servers, and are therefore reasonably secure. The service is still in beta, but it works well enough for daily use.

Pentesting Firmware

Hacking, and specifically malware always fascinated me. Early on I decided that I want to understand how exactly these malicious files work, and how to detect them. Throughout my teens I have analysed, decompiled and even written a few myself as a proof-of-concept. Now I am the creator of the Firmware called Xtreme for the Flipper Zero.
